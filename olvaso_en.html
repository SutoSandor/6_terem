Rosary or prayer beads


It is the artefact of Catholic worship and the veneration of Mary to count the repeated prayers on the occasion of private devotion. Based on Christian symbolism its name refers to Mary (the rose is the queen of flowers, Mary is the rose of secret meaning). According to tradition the first rosaries were made by Saint Dominic from pressed rose petals (for Virgin Mary as a rose wreath). At the occasion of the Fatima appearances, Virgin Mary asked to pray the Rosary every day for the conversion of sinners. The most common form of today's rosary is a string of “beads” made from wood, pearl shell, gall, metal, etc. with a cross at the beginning. The prayer Hail Mary is said on the so-called small beads, and the prayers Our Father, Glory Be and O Jesus are said on the large (old) beads separating the small ones. The small rosary consists of five decades (50 beads), and the large one consists of 15 (150 beads).
